BPTracker

Blood Pressure Tracker

Features
Record and Track Blood Pressure Readings

Super easy to record and observe blood pressure readings with contextual information about the readings.

Supports Dark Mode and Dynamic Types

App supports Dark Mode and Dynamic Font Sizes for better readability and customisation

Review readings using Charts

Use chart view to gain insights into BP readings.

Share readings with Apple Health

Integrates with Apple Health, allowing you to share your BP readings with Apple Health, allowing you to further share your BP readings with your loved ones and care providers using Apple Health platfrom.
